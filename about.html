About Us

Study Australia is an online money lending service dedicated to students who would like to pursue higher education. Our loan offers are suitable for international students looking to study in Australia and domestic students looking to study abroad. There are other forms of financial aids, such as scholarships and federal grants, to assist continuing education. Even so, some students still need additional financial assistance, which leads them to taking out student loans to fill in the gap. The loans we offer are designed to this specific need, and we make sure that we come up with a process that will streamline your application and repayments.

Consultation

Know all the information and requirements you need to prepare yourself to apply. Have a chat with us, and we’ll help you estimate your school and housing costs and other expenses.

Transparency

We do not encourage you to borrow more than what you need. Part of our process is looking into your education expenses, repayment history and possible future earnings. We calculate according to what you can afford and your potential.

Study Australia will assist you in paying your tuition fees and cost of living.
