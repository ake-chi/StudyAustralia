What is a student loan?

A student loan is a type of loan dedicated to helping you pay for higher education and the fees associated with it, such as course fees, books and accommodation. Student loans are affordatble, flexible and have lower interest rates. They are tailored to fund your education needs, and you can have the option to start the repayments once you are receiving your regular income.

What are the benefits of student loans?

Student loans have flexible terms and conditions, making it manageable for you to pay. They have deferred payment options and the interest rates are set accordingly. Taking out student loans also gives you the perfect opportunity to build a good credit score, which is an advantage for future financial transactions.
